# HIPAA Readiness Checklist

Use this only when the product creates, stores, processes, or transmits PHI.

## Data Handling

- PHI classification documented
- Minimum necessary access enforced
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Retention and deletion rules defined
- Redaction strategy for logs, prompts, and support tooling

## Vendor and Access Controls

- Business Associate Agreement requirement identified
- Approved subprocessors listed
- Workforce access policy documented
- Break-glass access rules documented
- Session and credential management defined

## Operational Controls

- Audit trail for PHI access
- Incident response for disclosure events
- Backup, restore, and availability process
- Training or usage constraints for operators and agents

## Gate

Do not move forward without confirming whether PHI enters prompts, memory, logs, or tool payloads.
